About Us

EDR Group started the Structural Design Consultancy Services since January 2012 under the name of Engineering Project Consultants a proprietorship firm and further established as Engineering Design and Research Pvt. Ltd. a company incorporated under Companies Act 1956 on 18th December 2012. Engineering Design and Research Pvt. Ltd. is a company providing the structural design consultancy services to clients in India and abroad. The company gets hold of well-to-do technical knowledge since day of inception through there associated people. The people associated with company have delivered the large and complex nature of design projects in India and abroad.

Our Strengths



The Organization had developed a strong culture that emphasized superior technical ability, in field of Structural Engineering. EDR Group is distinct in designing stable and economical structures without sacrificing architectural intend, aesthetics and functionality. EDR Group assures the commitments made for time, economy and quality. Our main objective is to deliver the superior quality product to the client. we have became a reputed name in the market because of the fact that all our designs are compliant with predefined standard specifications.

Client Satisfaction



The principal objective is to evolve Innovative and Cost Effective Solutions to problems encountered in Design and Construction Engineering fields and offer the most appropriate solutions to meet the clients specification. The firm offers total Engineering Design, Design and Construction Project Management Services including Design Management, Review of Design including Value Engineering, Continuous Technical Supervision at site for Quality Assurance and Progress Control and Contract Administration at all stages of works.
