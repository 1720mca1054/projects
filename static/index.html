Structural Design Consultancy - Engineering Design and Research Pvt. Ltd

Engineering Design and Research Pvt. Ltd. is a company providing the structural design consultancy services in India and globally. Virtually this group started the consulting services since January 2012 under the name of Engineering Project Consultants a proprietorship firm and further established as Engineering Design and Research Pvt. Ltd. a company incorporated under Companies Act 1956 on 18th December 2012. The company gets hold of well-to-do technical knowledge since day of inception through there associated people. The people associated with company have delivered the large and complex nature of design projects in India and abroad.

The company is founded by practicing structural engineer Dr. Santosh Kumar Singh, Ph.D (Structure) from IIT Kharagpur with 18 years of excellent experience in design and detailing of steel/RCC structures. Prior to establishment of Engineering Design and Research Pvt. Ltd., he has worked at Sr. Management Level in premier National and International organizations and delivered various projects of large and complex nature in India and abroad.

Skilled & Experienced Team

EDR Group has a highly skilled and experienced team of engineers. As the frontiers of structural engineering advance, our rapidly accumulating resource of knowledge, experience and expertise equip us not only for refined conventional design but also, to investigate, develop and use designs beyond the boundaries of convention.
